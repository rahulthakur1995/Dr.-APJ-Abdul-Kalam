APJ Abdul Kalaam

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam
(1931 - 2015)

The 'Indian Missile Man' Dr APJ Abdul Kalam was a world-renowned scientist, researcher, academic, and politician. Dr A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, India's 11th President, was a forerunner of the country's civilian space program as well as its defence and military missile development efforts. From a young age, he fought tooth and nail to complete his education and support his joint family, despite coming from a poor family.
